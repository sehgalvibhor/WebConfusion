Now we will design a simple webpage,just remember that the webpage is like your book page and consists of a title in it's head and content in it's body. 
    <html>
<head>
	<title>Hello World</title> 
</head>
<body>
	<h1>Hello World!!</h1>
	<p>This is my first simple webpage</p>
</body>
</html>
The words that are surrounded with angle brackets (< >) are called elements.
Write the above code in any Text Editor and save it as (any-name-of-your-choice).html
Now open up this page in your browser

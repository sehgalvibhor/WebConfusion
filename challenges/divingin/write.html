Know Hows

Typing anything in the write activity is as easy as writing anything on a paper.

  • Just open the Write Activity and start typing in it.
  • Type anything in the activity and Press Ctrl+S to the save your text.
  • A Dialog box will appear something like this:
  • Click on the rectangular box in front of 'Save file as type' and select HTML/PHTML/.. and click on save.
  • Now open your journal and hover over the file you had saved.
  • Now hover over 'open with' and select browser.
  • Hooorraay!! You can see your text in the browser.
